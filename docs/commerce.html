FILM

Experience acquired on the film set translates into the realization of modern, professional music clips and promotional films. The eye-catching visual is one of the strongest ways of advertising - it's important for it to be made creatively and professional. Each realization has its own unique style while fulfilling the client's expectations.

MUSIC

Stefan Monin's body of music work makes him uniquely suited to the filmmaking style. While searching for the formal relationship between the beats of the music he chooses how tethered the music need to be to the film. His work implies to intuitively work around the visual beats of the film - finding the truthful and unique voice within the frames of Your film.
