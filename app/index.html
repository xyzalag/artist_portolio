About

STEFAN MONIN

Stefan Monin (1994) born in Riwne - independent film director, scriptwriter, visual artist, music producer and founder of underground music label - Skinny Souls Records. Interested in interdisciplinary approach to art. He studied the Film Directing at AMA Film School (Multi Art Academy) in Krakow (2013-2016), graduating from the Faculty of Film and currently is finishing his postgraduate course.
Currently he is working on his second feature film, while also expanding the Skinny Souls brand.
