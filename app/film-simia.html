SIMIA

SIMIA is a 30 minute body horror written and directed by Stefan Monin.
It's a film where the main role is the stuffy atmosphere of the revenge cinema. The main character is guided by the primitive, brutal instincts engraved in his consciousness after traumatic experiences.
SIMIA is a cinema of the body - under the thick surface of wild, film violence, hidden secrets of human nature and humanity. Each frame of the film is sliced in meat - whether mature or twisted - but always exposed to all kinds of injuries and deformities. For the purest, physiological horror.
