STEFAN MONIN

Film Music

Stefan Monin's sound work in cinema investigates the use of a film space, through ambient soundscapes, that create works that ponder subtle transformations of the frame.
Rolling waves of noise, trebly drones emerging from the clouds of sonic abyss, expansive yet claustrophobic atmosphere of the seasons.
All of that as a part of haunted recordings made to create a piece of cinema for the ear.
