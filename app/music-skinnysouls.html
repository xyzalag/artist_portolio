STEFAN MONIN

Skinny Souls Records

SKINNY SOULS RECORDS is a small, independent label releasing experimental, cloudy and self-produced music founded by Stefan Monin, who serves as a producer and songwriter. The main domain of label is a total independence in the fields of recording, producing and releasing it's music.
Multi-disciplonary being the core of the label gives an emphasis on adaption to the rapidly changing cultural conditions of contemporary music.
With it's base in Kraków, Poland - Skinny Souls Records attempts to reflect the innovative nature of the unique music scene here, through releasing high quality recordings.
It is a non-profit organization, so all of it's income is invested in new records and support for the artists.
